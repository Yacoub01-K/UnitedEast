About United East Trading

Established in 1993, United East Trading has grown to become a leading force in the paper and board industry, known for our unwavering commitment to quality and innovation.

Global Network

As a trusted partner to manufacturers worldwide, we've built an extensive network spanning Asia and Europe. Our strategic partnerships with industry leaders enable us to:

  • Source premium products from China, India, Indonesia, and Europe
  • Deliver competitive advantages through established relationships
  • Ensure reliable supply chains across international markets

Technical Excellence

Our expertise extends beyond traditional trading. We provide comprehensive solutions including:

  • Strategic sourcing and supplier evaluation
  • Rigorous quality control and testing
  • Optimized logistics and supply chain management
  • Technical consultation and product specifications

Industry Recognition

Our commitment to excellence has earned us multiple prestigious awards from Asia Pulp and Paper (APP):

  • Outstanding Contribution in Promoting Carbonless and Preprint
  • Commitment and Dedication to Excellence
  • Highest Sales Achievement in Jordan Market
